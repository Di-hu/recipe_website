White Sauce Pasta Recipe

White Sauce Pasta

Learn how to make a creamy and delicious white sauce pasta that will satisfy your cravings!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic.
  3. Pour in cream and milk, stirring constantly.
  4. Mix in the boiled pasta and cheese.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.
