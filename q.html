Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, or basil) for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water. In a medium pot, combine quinoa and water (or vegetable broth). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cover. Simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the vegetables. Slice the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, and thinly slice the red onion.
  3. Once quinoa is ready, transfer to a large bowl and let it cool slightly.
  4. Add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and avocado to the quinoa.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, and season with salt and pepper. Toss gently to combine.
  6. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve immediately, or chill in the refrigerator for a refreshing cold bowl.
