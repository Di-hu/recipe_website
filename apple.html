Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients

For the Pie Crust

  • 2 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup ice water

For the Apple Filling

  • 6-8 medium apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, mixing until dough forms. Divide into two equal portions, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. For the filling, peel, core, and slice the apples. In a separate bowl, mix sugar, brown sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and flour. Toss the apple slices in the mixture until evenly coated.
  4. Roll out one dough portion to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place the rolled dough in the pie dish and add the apple filling. Dot with butter.
  5. Roll out the second dough portion and place it over the apples. Trim excess dough and crimp the edges to seal. Cut a few slits in the top to allow steam to escape.
  6. Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly. Let cool before serving.
