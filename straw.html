Strawberry Shortcake Recipe

Ingredients

For the Shortcake

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream, plus extra for brushing
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Strawberries

  • 1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the Whipped Cream

  • 1 cup heavy cream, chilled
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix sliced strawberries with 1/4 cup sugar and let them sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the cold butter.
  4. Mix cream, egg, and vanilla and add to dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  5. Pat dough into a rectangle, cut into rounds, and bake for 12-15 minutes until golden.
  6. Whip cream, sugar, and vanilla until soft peaks form.
  7. Assemble by slicing biscuits, adding strawberries and whipped cream, and topping with the biscuit half.
