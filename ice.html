Iced Coffee Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee (chilled)
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • 1-2 teaspoons sugar or syrup (optional)
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Brew a cup of coffee and let it cool to room temperature, or chill it in the refrigerator.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes, then pour the chilled coffee over the ice.
  3. Add milk or cream to taste, and stir in sugar or syrup if desired.
  4. Top with whipped cream if you'd like an extra indulgent touch.
  5. Enjoy your refreshing Iced Coffee!
