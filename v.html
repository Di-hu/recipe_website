Vegetable Stir-fry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds for garnish (optional)
  • Cooked rice or noodles (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced onion and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Add bell pepper, zucchini, broccoli, and carrot. Stir-fry for 5-6 minutes, or until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in soy sauce, hoisin sauce (optional), and sesame oil. Stir to coat the vegetables evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir-fry for an additional 2 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and serve over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds, if desired.
