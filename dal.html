Dal Batti Recipe

Traditional Dal Batti

A hearty and flavorful Rajasthani dish made with spiced lentils (dal) and baked wheat dough balls (batti). Perfect for a satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup split pigeon peas (toor dal)
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dal: Wash the dal and cook it with turmeric, salt, and water until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter. Add red chili powder and cooked dal. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Make the Batti: Knead wheat flour with a pinch of salt and ghee to make a stiff dough. Shape into small balls and bake at 180°C (350°F) until golden brown.
  4. Once baked, dip each batti in melted ghee for extra flavor.
  5. Serve the dal with batti and garnish with fresh coriander.
