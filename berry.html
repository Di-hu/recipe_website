Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 banana (for added creaminess)
  • Ice cubes (optional, if a thicker smoothie is desired)

Instructions

  1. Add blueberries, yogurt, milk, honey or maple syrup, and banana to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. Add ice cubes if desired for a thicker consistency.
  3. Taste and add more honey or maple syrup if additional sweetness is needed.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy your refreshing Blueberry Smoothie!
