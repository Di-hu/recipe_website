Lemon Meringue Pie Recipe

Ingredients

For the Crust

  • 1 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2-3 tablespoons ice water

For the Lemon Filling

  • 1 and 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups water
  • 4 large egg yolks, lightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest

For the Meringue

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. For the crust, combine flour, sugar, salt, and butter in a food processor until crumbly. Add ice water and pulse until dough forms. Roll out and fit into a 9-inch pie dish. Bake for 15 minutes, then cool.
  3. For the filling, combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt in a saucepan. Gradually add water and cook over medium heat until thickened. Stir in egg yolks, butter, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Pour into cooled crust.
  4. For the meringue, beat egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form. Spread meringue over the pie, sealing the edges.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until the meringue is golden. Cool before serving.
