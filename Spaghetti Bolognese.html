Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan over medium heat and add ground beef. Cook until browned, then drain excess fat.
  2. Add chopped onion, garlic, carrot, and celery to the pan. Cook for 5-6 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, and basil. If using, pour in red wine and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add beef broth and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the spaghetti and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.
