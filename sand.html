Classic Sandwich Recipe

About This Sandwich

This classic sandwich recipe is perfect for a quick and delicious meal! It's versatile and customizable, making it a favorite for everyone. Fill it with fresh veggies, cheese, and your favorite spreads for a flavorful bite every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole grain, or your choice)
  • 2-3 slices of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your favorite)
  • 4-5 slices of tomato
  • 4-5 slices of cucumber
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • Butter or mayo for spreading
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Spread butter or mayo on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Layer the cheese, tomato slices, cucumber slices, and lettuce on one slice of bread.
  3. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper for added flavor.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side down.
  5. For a warm sandwich, toast it in a skillet until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Slice the sandwich in half and enjoy!
