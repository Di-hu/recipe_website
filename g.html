Grilled Salmon Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional, for a touch of sweetness)
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, etc.) for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 375°F - 400°F).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Optionally, add honey for a sweet glaze.
  3. Brush the salmon fillets with the olive oil mixture, ensuring both sides are coated.
  4. Place the salmon fillets on the grill, skin-side down, and grill for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Once the salmon is done, remove it from the grill and drizzle with fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of parsley.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra fresh herbs if desired.
