Grilled Chicken Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 165°F or 75°C).
  4. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the salad by combining mixed greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and red onion in a large bowl.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, slice it into strips and add to the salad.
  6. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over the salad, and toss to combine.
  7. Top with feta cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
