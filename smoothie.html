Fruit Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • Toppings: fresh fruits, granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine frozen berries, banana, almond milk, and Greek yogurt.
  2. Blend until smooth and thick, adding a little more milk if needed to blend.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Top with fresh fruit, granola, chia seeds, and coconut flakes as desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your healthy smoothie bowl!
