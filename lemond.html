Mint Lemonade Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1/4 cup honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 lemon, sliced (for garnish)
  • Extra mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup of water with honey or sugar. Heat over medium heat until dissolved, then let it cool.
  2. In a blender, add mint leaves and lemon juice. Add the cooled syrup and blend until smooth.
  3. Pour the mixture into a large pitcher. Add the remaining 3 cups of cold water and mix well.
  4. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the mint lemonade over the ice.
  5. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh mint leaves.
  6. Serve and enjoy your refreshing Mint Lemonade!
