Delicious Idli Recipe

About Idli

Idli is a soft and fluffy South Indian steamed rice cake, popular as a breakfast dish. Made from a fermented rice and lentil batter, it's served with sambar, chutney, or podi, making it both healthy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice (preferably idli rice)
  • 1/4 cup split black gram (urad dal)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and urad dal separately. Soak rice with fenugreek seeds and urad dal in separate bowls for at least 4 hours.
  2. Grind the urad dal with some water into a smooth, fluffy batter. Similarly, grind the rice into a slightly coarse batter.
  3. Mix both batters together in a large bowl, add salt, and let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  4. Grease the idli molds, pour the batter, and steam for about 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or any condiment of your choice.
